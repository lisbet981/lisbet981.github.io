Spell Bound Tempo,
I focused I implemented directional player movement using sprites for up, down, left, and right,
along with coded attack and shield mechanics triggered by mouse input—left click for attacking
and right click for shielding. These systems controlled player states such as walking, attacking,
and defending, ensuring smooth transitions between animations and actions.
